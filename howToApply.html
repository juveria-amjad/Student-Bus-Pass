How to Apply

The student bus passes are offered in two types:

  1. General Bus Pass
  2. Exclusive Bus Pass
The services offered are to apply, renew and modify these passes.

Things to Remember

Few things to keep in mind while applying for a bus pass:

  • Once submitted, students CAN NOT modify their PDF uploads, institution, gender and payment details.
  • All the students are required to upload their latest bonafide certificates.
  • Any invalid/wrong information will lead to rejection of the appplication.
  • Online Payment option is currently unavailable.
  • Students are advised to save their Application ID for future purposes.
  • Changes in application will not be allowed after 26-Oct-2021.
  • The bus passes will be issued within 2 weeks from 26-Oct-2021 and students will be notified accordingly.

Frequently asked questions

Can we cancel our application on the website?

No. There is no previlege to cancel the application online. You can email us at bus-support@vasavi.com or call us on +91 12 4567 9890.

Will the bus stop at Charminar?

The bus routes will be confirmed after receiving all the applications and the respective bus numbers and the drivers' contact information will be shared when the bus pass is issued.

How will we know when the bus passes are issued?

A mail will be sent to the email id's specified in the form. So, please make sure to use an active mail id.
